Strawberry Ice Cream

Description

The sweet-tart burst of ripe, juicy strawberries stirred into sweet, frozen cream is a summer pleasure unlike any other. It's light and refreshing and tastes like summer on a spoon.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups mashed fresh strawberries
  • 2 drops red food coloring (Optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the milk, cream, sugar, salt, vanilla, strawberries, and food coloring. Pour the mixture into the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker, and freeze according to manufacturer's directions.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 354 calories; protein 3.6g; carbohydrates 34.1g; fat 23.4g; cholesterol 86.4mg; sodium 121mg.
